The Mission

Education Not Incarceration is about giving information to the general population on why education of all forms is so important to our prison systems. Education in prison can help men and women prepare for a successful release and reduce the probability of recidivism. The link between incarceration and lack of education is strong. According to the Bureau of Justice Statistics, two-thirds of state prison inmates have not completed high school. Let's change how our society looks at prisons.

"Mass incarceration does not make us safer. It denies our nation the productivity and creativity of millions of our people."
